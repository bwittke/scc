Training

Weekly Workouts

Cloud City Track Club offers two group workouts a week: a weekday speed workout and a weekend long run workout. To account for busy schedules, we offer two different practice times for weekday workouts.

If you are interested in trying out a workout, please fill out this form and we will be in touch with you shortly. Existing club members can find weekly workout details posted in the Team Slack.

Member Time Standards

We work with runners that are interested in taking their running to the next level, and prefer our club members to have met at least one of the following race standards:

  • 5k: 25:00
  • 10k: 52:00
  • Half Marathon: 1:55
  • Marathon: 4:00

    • NOTE: If you do not meet any of the time standards but do have at least 2 years of experience running on a regular basis, please talk to us! We would still love to work with you to achieve your goals.
