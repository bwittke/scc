Who We Are

Cloud City Track Club was founded in December 2023 in Seattle, Washington, the ultimate Cloud City.

Our members love training, competing, team spirit, and having fun! We strive to create a team that works toward similar competitive goals while also engaging with the broader running community in the Seattle-area and beyond!

Our Goals

We strive to provide high quality training for track and field, road running, and mountain/ultra/trail running through a fun, inclusive team atmosphere where athletes feel supported. We also provide an athlete centric environment, meaning our funds go toward supporting our athletes and their competitive goals.
